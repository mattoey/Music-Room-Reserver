My Reservations
Please read all Regulations
  • Recurring reservations are no longer available.
  • The student who reserves the space is responsible and accountable for ensuring appropriate use of the facility.
  • If your reservation is confirmed, you will be directed as to when and where to meet a staff member or collect a key to open the space.
  • You will need to provide the staff person with a student ID card or driver's license to hold while you are using the space.
  • Be ready to leave on time.
  • To ensure security do not leave the space unattended while the door is unlocked.
  • This space is intended for use by Stevenson students to practice music/performance that would otherwise be disruptive in the residence halls due to noise levels.
  • Though this separate enclosed space is helpful in reducing noise impact to others, it is possible that noise complaints may happen. If a student or staff person asks you to reduce volume, please do so immediately or stop using the space.
  • At this time, we are not able to offer instrument storage.
  • The practice space currently has the following equipment (although this listing is not a guarantee of availability): drum kit; Yamaha YPT-230 keyboard; Drive bass amp; Johnson guitar amp; two microphones; Phonic 410 100w powered mixer; two monitors.
  • University, college, and housing rules apply. Violations will be subject to standard sanctions in addition to loss of privileges for the practice space.
