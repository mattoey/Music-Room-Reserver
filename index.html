Josh Alper Marines Music Room

The Josh Alper Marines Music Room is a program initiated due to the requests of students and the collaboration and effort of Stevenson staff members.  The space is located in the small building off of the Stevenson service road, behind House 3.  The facility is conducive to 1-6 individuals. 

Thanks for your help in making this room successful!

Please reserve the space at least one day in advance (no same day reservations or over weekends). 

  • This space is for the use of Stevenson affiliates only.
  • Please, no drugs and alcohol in the practice room -- you will lose privileges and endanger the program for everyone if you do not respect these policies.
